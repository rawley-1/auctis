SECTION: Governing legal standard

CASE: United Food & Commercial Workers Union v. Zuckerberg
COURT: Delaware Supreme Court
YEAR: 2021
DOCTRINE: Demand futility
AUTHORITY: Binding precedent
KEY TOPIC: Director independence, liability exposure, board analysis

Accordingly, from this point forward, courts should ask the following three questions on a director-by-director basis when evaluating allegations of demand futility: ...

(i) whether the director received a material personal benefit...
(ii) whether the director faces a substantial likelihood of liability...
(iii) whether the director lacks independence...

If the answer to any of the questions is “yes” for at least half of the members of the demand board, then demand is excused as futile...
