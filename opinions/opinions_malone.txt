CASE: Malone v. Brincat
COURT: Delaware Supreme Court
DOCTRINE_LINE: disclosure
CASE_ROLE: foundation


RULE_EXCERPTS:
Directors must communicate truthfully with stockholders.


APPLICATION_EXCERPTS:
Misleading statements to shareholders can breach fiduciary duties even without a request for shareholder action.


FACTS:
Directors allegedly made misleading public statements about the company.


HOLDING:
Disclosure violations can independently constitute a breach of fiduciary duty.


NOTES:
Key case for standalone disclosure duty.
