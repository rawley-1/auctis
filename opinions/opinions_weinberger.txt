CASE: Weinberger v. UOP
COURT: Delaware Supreme Court
DOCTRINE_LINE: entire_fairness
CASE_ROLE: foundation


RULE_EXCERPTS:
The concept of fairness has two basic aspects: fair dealing and fair price.


APPLICATION_EXCERPTS:
The court evaluated both the negotiation process and the financial terms to determine whether the transaction was entirely fair to minority shareholders.


FACTS:
A parent corporation acquired its subsidiary through a merger, raising concerns about fairness to minority stockholders.


HOLDING:
Entire fairness is the governing standard where fiduciaries stand on both sides of the transaction.


NOTES:
Foundational case defining fair dealing and fair price.
