CASE: Schnell v. Chris-Craft
COURT: Delaware Supreme Court
DOCTRINE_LINE: equitable_intervention
CASE_ROLE: foundation


RULE_EXCERPTS:
Inequitable action does not become permissible simply because it is legally possible.


APPLICATION_EXCERPTS:
The court blocked the board’s manipulation of meeting timing.


FACTS:
Board changed meeting date to disadvantage dissidents.


HOLDING:
Equity intervenes to prevent abuse of corporate machinery.


NOTES:
Classic equity-over-form case.
