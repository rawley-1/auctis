CASE: Blasius Industries v. Atlas
COURT: Delaware Chancery Court
DOCTRINE_LINE: shareholder_franchise
CASE_ROLE: foundation


RULE_EXCERPTS:
Interference with the shareholder franchise requires a compelling justification.


APPLICATION_EXCERPTS:
The board’s action to increase board size and dilute voting power was scrutinized.


FACTS:
Board acted to block shareholder influence over corporate direction.


HOLDING:
Board actions interfering with voting rights are subject to strict review.


NOTES:
Core case protecting shareholder voting rights.
